`hanja_gloss.txt` is **derived** by `scripts/build_gloss_wiktionary.py`: for each single Hanja that
libhangul `hanja.txt` leaves WITHOUT a 훈음, the first-sense 뜻 is taken from the Korean Wiktionary 한자
entry and the reading (a factual pronunciation, from the libhangul inventory) is appended to form
`읽기:한자:훈음` (e.g. 椵 → `나무 이름 가`). Only the gloss body is the copyrightable Wiktionary content;
example sentences, etymology, and multimedia are not read. This data stays under **CC BY-SA** (ShareAlike)
— NOT the MIT license.
